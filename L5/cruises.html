    Loyal Caribbean's luxurious voyages from Marina Bay Cruise Centre feature Voyager of the Seas, an ultra-deluxe ship with surfing simulators, rock-climbing, ice skating, and more. Cruises span 3 days to a week, visiting Malaysia and Thailand. Book on their website.

    Americano Cruise Line

    Americano Jade, refreshed with new amenities, offers 6-8 day cruises to Phuket, Langkawi, and Penang from Singapore. Longer cruises cover usual stops and extend to unique destinations like the Maldives, Seychelles, Oman, and the UAE. Limited seats; book early on their website.
